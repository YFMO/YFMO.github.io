Yanfang Mo

 

Dr. Yanfang Mo will be a Research Assistant Professor with ECE, HKUST from March 2024 and is currently a Postdoc with HKIDS, CityU. I received a PhD degree in Electronic and Computer Engineering at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, supervised by Prof. Li Qiu. I was a postdoc at City University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. S. Joe Qin, Prof. Minghua Chen, and Prof. Lishuai Li.

E-mail: ymoaa@connect.ust.hk

Research Interest

My research interests include data-driven modeling and decision-making in smart grids, intelligent transportation, and finance. I have been developing my knowledge in data analytics, statistical learning, online and combinatorial optimization, partial order programming, matrix theory, graph theory, game theory, and control theory, etc. I am interested in building theoretical foundations and conducting data-driven analysis toward smart cities.
